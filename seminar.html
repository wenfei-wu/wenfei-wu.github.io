NetLab@IIIS Members

    • collecting and assigning papers,
    • setting up the online and offline meeting room,
    • announcing the event 3 days ahead,
    • updating this page (the "Incoming Seminar" and "All seminars" fields).
  • Each group member should take one paper to present.

External Participants

  • You are welcome to listen; if you are willing to present (either your work or a paper), please contact with the organizer for the paper assignment.
  • You can subscribe and unsubscribe by email, and the event notification would be sent 3 days before the event.
  • Collaborations are welcome!
